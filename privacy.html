Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

At Lionel X ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you create an account:

  • Email Address: Used for account authentication and communication
  • Display Name (Callsign): Your chosen username displayed in the app
  • Avatar Initials: Generated from your display name for profile display
  • Access Code: Used during registration for account verification

1.2 Health and Fitness Data

We collect and process health-related information to provide our core services:

  • Step Count: Daily step data from your device's sensors or Health Connect/Google Fit
  • Distance Traveled: Calculated from your steps and active sessions
  • Calories Burned: Estimated based on your activity and body metrics
  • Body Metrics: Height, weight, age, and biological sex (optional, for accurate calorie calculations)
  • Active Session Data: Duration, pace, speed, and route information during tracked workouts

1.3 Location Data

We collect precise location data only when you use the Active Session tracking feature:

  • GPS Coordinates: Real-time location during active tracking sessions
  • Route Maps: Stored as a sequence of coordinates to display your workout paths
  • Permission Required: Location tracking requires explicit user consent and can be disabled at any time

1.4 Usage and Performance Data

  • App Activity: Leaderboard interactions, protocol task completions, audiobook progress
  • Streak Data: Current streak, longest streak, and daily target achievements
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous error logs to improve app stability (no personal data included)

1.5 Notification Preferences

  • Push notification tokens for sending reminders and updates
  • Notification settings and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Track and display your daily step count, distance, and calories
  • Calculate and maintain your streak data
  • Display your progress on daily, weekly, and monthly dashboards
  • Enable GPS tracking during active workout sessions
  • Save audiobook bookmarks and playback progress

2.2 Leaderboard and Social Features

  • Display your ranking on global leaderboards (if you opt in)
  • Show your display name, avatar initials, and step count to other users
  • You can hide yourself from leaderboards at any time in Privacy Settings

2.3 Personalization

  • Customize daily step goals based on your preferences
  • Send personalized notifications and reminders
  • Provide tailored progress insights

2.4 Communication

  • Send important app updates and feature announcements
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Notify you about streak milestones and achievements

2.5 Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyze app usage patterns to improve performance
  • Fix bugs and resolve technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user behavior

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our app:

  • Supabase: Our backend database and authentication provider. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Supabase is GDPR compliant and SOC 2 Type II certified.
    Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
  • Health Connect / Google Fit: Used to read step and activity data from your device. We only request read permissions and never write data back to these services.

3.2 Public Leaderboards

If you enable leaderboard visibility (opt-in), the following information is visible to other users:

  • Display name (callsign)
  • Avatar initials
  • Step count
  • Leaderboard rank

Note: You can hide yourself from leaderboards at any time in Profile → Privacy & Security.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

3.4 What We DO NOT Do

  • ❌ We DO NOT sell your personal data to third parties
  • ❌ We DO NOT share your data for marketing purposes
  • ❌ We DO NOT use your data for advertising
  • ❌ We DO NOT share your location data with anyone

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS/SSL encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: All data stored in our database is encrypted
  • Secure Authentication: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (never stored in plain text)
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can view your data
  • Regular Security Audits: We perform regular security reviews and updates

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained indefinitely while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Backup Data: Backup copies are automatically deleted within 90 days
  • Legal Requirements: We may retain certain information if required by law

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 Access and Portability

  • Export Your Data: Go to Profile → Privacy & Security → Export My Data
  • Receive a JSON file containing all your data (steps, sessions, profile, streaks, bookmarks)

6.2 Correction

  • Update your display name, body metrics, and preferences at any time in the app

6.3 Deletion

  • Delete Your Account: Go to Profile → Privacy & Security → Delete Account
  • This will permanently delete:
    • Your profile and authentication credentials
    • All step history and daily records
    • All active session data and routes
    • Streak data and achievements
    • Audiobook bookmarks
    • All other associated data

6.4 Restrict Processing

  • Disable Location Tracking: Turn off Active Session tracking
  • Hide from Leaderboards: Toggle "Show on Leaderboard" off in Privacy Settings
  • Disable Notifications: Manage notification preferences in app settings

6.5 Object to Processing

You can contact us at info@outstandr.com to object to how we process your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: Request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information (with certain exceptions)
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information, so no opt-out is necessary
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

10. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal Basis for Processing: We process your data based on your consent, contractual necessity, and legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent at any time (this will not affect prior processing)
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You can file a complaint with your local data protection authority
  • Data Protection Officer: Contact info@outstandr.com for GDPR-related inquiries
