The Lionel X mobile application is owned and operated by LEADERS PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT CONSULTANCIES L.L.C, a limited liability company registered in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

At Lionel X ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you create an account:

  • Email Address: Used for account authentication and secure communication.
  • Display Name (Callsign): Your chosen username displayed on dashboards.
  • Avatar Initials: Generated from your display name for profile identification.
  • Verification Code: Used during registration to verify physical book ownership or access rights.

1.2 Health and Fitness Data (Read-Only)

We integrate with Apple HealthKit and Google Health Connect to verify your discipline protocols. We only READ this data; we do not write data back to your health apps.

  • Step Count: Read daily to verify 10,000-step targets.
  • Distance & Calories: Calculated locally based on your activity metrics.
  • Body Metrics: Height, weight, and biological sex (optional) are used solely for calorie accuracy.

1.3 Location Data (Active Sessions Only)

We collect precise location data only when you explicitly start an "Active Session" tracking feature:

  • GPS Coordinates: Collected in real-time to map your route and calculate distance.
  • Background Tracking: Location is tracked if you lock your screen during a walk, but tracking stops immediately when you end the session.
  • Permission Required: You can disable location permissions at any time in your device settings.

1.4 Usage and Device Data

  • App Activity: Leaderboard interactions, protocol task completions, audiobook progress.
  • Streak Data: Current streak, longest streak, and daily target achievements.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version (Android/iOS), and app version.

2. Data Retention Policy

We enforce strict data retention limits to minimize your digital footprint:

  • Location Data (GPS): Retained for 90 days. Detailed GPS route data is automatically deleted from our servers 90 days after the session is created.
  • Step History: Indefinite Retention. Daily summaries (e.g., "Jan 12: 10,500 steps") are kept indefinitely to maintain your streak history and lifetime stats, unless you delete your account.
  • Account Data: Retained until you request account deletion. Upon deletion, data is removed immediately.

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We use trusted third-party infrastructure to operate our app. These providers are authorized data processors:

  • Lovable Cloud / Supabase (Backend & Database): We use Lovable Cloud and Supabase as our primary backend infrastructure to store user profiles, step summaries, and authentication data. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Notifications): We use Google Firebase solely for delivering push notifications (e.g., "Target Hit" or "Streak Reminder"). We do not use Firebase for analytics or advertising tracking.
  • Google Health Connect / Apple HealthKit: We interface with these services solely to read step counts. We do not share your data back to them.

3.2 Public Leaderboards (Opt-In)

If you enable leaderboard visibility, the following is visible to other users:

  • Display Name (Callsign)
  • Total Step Count
  • Global Rank

Note: You can hide yourself from leaderboards instantly in Profile → Privacy & Security.

3.3 What We DO NOT Do

  • ❌ We DO NOT sell your personal data.
  • ❌ We DO NOT share GPS location history with advertisers.
  • ❌ We DO NOT use your data for marketing purposes outside of app functionality.

4. Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Secure Authentication: Passwords are never stored in plain text; they are hashed using industry-standard algorithms.
  • Access Control: Strict role-based access limits internal access to your data.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Under the GDPR and CCPA, you have specific rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you (JSON export).
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate profile data directly in the app.
  • Right to Erasure: Request permanent deletion of your account.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Disable location tracking or leaderboard participation.

6. Data Deletion & Account Management

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion:

  1. In-App (Instant): Open Lionel X → Profile → Privacy & Security → Delete Account. This action is immediate.
  2. Email Request: Contact us at info@outstandr.com with the subject "Delete My Account".

What Happens When You Delete:

  • Profile and authentication credentials are wiped.
  • All step history and streak records are permanently removed.
  • You are removed from all leaderboards.
  • Backup Retention: Residual data in encrypted backups may remain for up to 30 days before being overwritten.

Data Export (Before Deletion):

You can export your data before deletion:

  1. Open Lionel X app → Profile → Privacy & Security → Export Data
  2. Your data will be downloaded as a JSON file including profile, steps, sessions, and bookmarks.

7. Children's Privacy

Lionel X is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.
